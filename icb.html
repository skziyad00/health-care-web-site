Calories: Gain & Burn Guide (Age 2–40)

Activity Level Calories Needed Purpose
Low Activity 1,600–2,000 kcal Weight maintenance
Moderate Activity 2,000–2,500 kcal Healthy weight gain
High Activity 2,500–3,000+ kcal Muscle gain & energy
  • To gain weight: Eat more calories than you burn daily
  • To burn fat: Burn more calories through exercise
  • Choose nutrient-rich foods instead of junk calories
  • Regular physical activity improves calorie balance
  • Proper sleep helps regulate calorie usage
