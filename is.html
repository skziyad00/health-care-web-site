Daily Sleep Requirement (Age 2–40)

Age Group Recommended Sleep
2–5 Years 10–13 Hours
6–10 Years 9–12 Hours
11–17 Years 8–10 Hours
18–30 Years 7–9 Hours
31–40 Years 7–8 Hours
  • Maintain a fixed sleep and wake-up time daily
  • Avoid screens at least 1 hour before bedtime
  • Sleep in a dark, quiet, and cool environment
  • Avoid caffeine and heavy meals at night
  • Good sleep improves memory, immunity, and mental health
  • Lack of sleep can cause stress, weight gain, and poor focus
