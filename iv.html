Daily Vitamin Requirements (General Guide)

Vitamin Recommended Intake Main Benefits
Vitamin A 700–900 mcg Eye health, immunity
Vitamin B-Complex Varies Energy, brain function
Vitamin C 65–90 mg Immunity, skin health
Vitamin D 600–800 IU Bone strength
Vitamin E 15 mg Antioxidant protection
Vitamin K 90–120 mcg Blood clotting
  • Get vitamins mainly from fruits, vegetables, and whole foods
  • Vitamin D can be obtained from sunlight exposure
  • Avoid excess vitamin supplements without medical advice
  • Balanced diet ensures proper vitamin absorption
