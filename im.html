Essential Minerals & Their Functions

Mineral Recommended Intake Main Function
Iron 8–18 mg Oxygen transport in blood
Magnesium 310–420 mg Muscle & nerve function
Zinc 8–11 mg Immunity & wound healing
Potassium 2,600–3,400 mg Heart & muscle function
Iodine 150 mcg Thyroid hormone production
  • Minerals help regulate body processes and metabolism
  • Iron deficiency can cause fatigue and weakness
  • Eat fruits, vegetables, nuts, and whole grains
  • Avoid excessive mineral supplements unless prescribed
