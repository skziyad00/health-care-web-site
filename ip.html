Daily Protein Requirement (Age 2–40)

Age Group Recommended Intake
2–5 Years 13–19 g
6–10 Years 19–34 g
11–18 Years 34–52 g
19–30 Years 46–56 g
31–40 Years 46–56 g
  • Protein is essential for muscle growth and repair
  • Good sources include eggs, milk, pulses, fish, and nuts
  • Protein supports immunity and hormone production
  • Excess protein without exercise may cause imbalance
